Groovy Bubbles
ABOUT
Help your bubble go with the flow, dodge bad vibes and achieve tranquility in this battle of Glob vs. Evil!

Use dexterity and light bulbs to manipulate the environment.
Guide your bubble towards peaceful blobs.
Avoid touching evil at all costs.
Collect enough peaceful blobs to achieve tranquility.
> Web demo not working? Try downloading the Windows copy! (or Linux )
CONTROLS
      Best experienced with a controller
  • WASD keys or LEFT-JOYSTICK to navigate menus.
  • WS keys or LEFT-JOYSTICK to control left heat source.
  • UP/DOWN keys or RIGHT-JOYSTICK to control right heat source.
  • BACKSPACE or START to pause
